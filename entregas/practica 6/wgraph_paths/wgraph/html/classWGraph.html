Detailed Description

template<class T>
class WGraph< T >

Models the Weighted Graph ADT. A Weighted graph is directed graph where all nodes are connected with all by default with infinity weight. Two nodes u,v are adjacent if the weight of the edge u->v is less than infinity.

Invariant
!is_empty() || size()==0
!is_full() || size()>=capacity()
